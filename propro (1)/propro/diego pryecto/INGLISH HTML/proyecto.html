PROJECT

1. FORMULATION

• It involves clearly defining your objectives, strategies and actions to develop a professional, satisfying and meaningful career that is aligned with your values, goals and personal tastes.

• According to magazines in the country, 6 out of 10 young people do not continue their studies after high school, why does this happen? In most cases, this happens because young people do not have enough financial resources to cover the cost of a private university and we know that entering a public university in Colombia is complicated, since everyone tries to enter these universities. Due to these problems, we would like to provide you with help where you can find where to access free higher education.

1. General: • We are looking for people to direct this project and our general objective is to help connect young people who are leaving high school to obtain better tools and knowledge free of charge and of quality with the purpose of giving them passage and vision of your professional future in a free and conscious way.

2. Specific: • Know your interests: Reflect on the activities and subjects that you enjoy most at school and in your free time. Consider the topics you work on and motivate you to continue researching.

3. Specific: • Try activities: Get involved in activities related to your diverse skills and performances in each academic area, this will provide us with the necessary information to link your results with what we believe is appropriate for you based on your knowledge.

4. Specific:• Assess your skills: Do a self-assessment of your educational strengths and weaknesses. We will consider carrying out aptitude tests and receiving feedback. With this we will move towards the direction used by them to find security in the career already suggested.

• Our problem deals with the indecision of young people aged 16 or over, students or already graduated, in making decisions about their studies.

• It helps you build your resume and your abilities to find a suitable career for you, this will provide you with the information corresponding to the careers and universities that you could enter according to your abilities and economic situation.
